Comments This contract is a call-up against a Public Works and Government Services Canada procurement tool. Dessau was hired through the standing offer to provide a competent professional mechanical engineer to review and sign the cooling tower Legionella Bacteria Control Management Programs (LBCMP) as required by the new MD 15161-2013 Control of Legionella in Mechanical Systems. This also permitted PWGSC to be in compliance with the new Health and Safety Provincial regulation for operating and maintaining cooling towers. The mandate was for 4 locations where PWGSC managed facilities with cooliong towers. The 4 locations were: 715 Peel in Montreal, 1001 St-Laurent in Longueuil, 3600 Casavant in St-Hyacinthe and 850 route de la mer in Ste-Flavie.
